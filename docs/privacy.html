Privacy Policy

⚠️ Prototype draft — finalize with counsel and confirm CCPA/CPRA obligations before launch.

What we collect

When you book or contact us, we collect your name, contact details, service address, and any notes you provide. Payment information is handled securely by Stripe; we don’t store card numbers.

How we use it

To schedule and deliver your cleaning, send confirmations and reminders, process payment, request reviews after service, and — with your consent — send occasional offers and re-booking reminders.

Sharing

We share information only with the assigned cleaner and service providers (e.g., Stripe, scheduling, SMS/email) needed to deliver the service. We don’t sell your personal information.

Your choices

You can opt out of marketing messages anytime, and request access to or deletion of your information by emailing us.
