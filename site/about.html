Built on showing up — and doing it right

We started Pacific Maid Co. because booking a cleaner in San Diego shouldn’t mean a form, a callback, and crossed fingers. You deserve a clear price, a real time slot, and someone you can trust in your home.

What makes us different

Most cleaning companies make you “request a quote” and wait. We show you the price up front and let you book online in about a minute. And once you’ve booked, we focus on the things that actually matter to you:

  • The same cleaner every visit. Recurring clients get a dedicated pro who learns your home, your preferences, and your pets.
  • Reliability you can set your watch by. We show up on time and send an “on my way” text. No no-shows.
  • Real vetting. Every cleaner is background-checked and employed by us (W-2) — not an anonymous gig worker.
  • Fully insured & bonded. For your protection and ours, with a 72-hour re-clean guarantee on every job.
  • No surprises. Flat-rate pricing, no hidden memberships, no junk fees.

Our promise

If something isn’t right, tell us within 72 hours and we’ll come back and re-clean it — free. That’s the whole policy. Simple.
